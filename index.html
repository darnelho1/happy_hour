One App for the Happiest of Hours

How cities have gone without a decent Happy Hour search app is beyond us, but we went ahead and made one just for you - the hard-working citizen on the hunt for a quality drink at a damn good price!

We aren't going to waste more hot air explaining what this app is good for. You already know! So jump in and start searching. While you do, we will keep making improvements. Cheers to good drinks, good food, good friends, and an app that actually works!
